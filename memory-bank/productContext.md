# Product Context: Open Runner

## Problem Solved

Open Runner provides a fun and accessible browser-based gaming experience that can be played on a variety of devices. It addresses the need for a simple, engaging, and infinitely replayable game that doesn't require installation or powerful hardware.

## How It Works

The game is an endless runner where the player's primary goal is to survive as long as possible while achieving a high score. The player navigates a procedurally generated landscape, avoiding static and dynamic obstacles.

- **Game Flow**: The player starts at a title screen, can select a level, and then begins the run. The game ends when the player collides with an obstacle or enemy. The player's score is based on the distance traveled and coins collected.
- **Player Experience**: The game is designed to be intuitive and easy to learn, with simple controls for turning. The increasing speed and variety of obstacles provide a progressive challenge. The visual and audio feedback for collecting items and colliding with objects enhances the experience.
- **User Interface**: The UI consists of a title screen, level selection, in-game HUD (score), and a game-over screen. Mobile-specific on-screen controls are also provided.
