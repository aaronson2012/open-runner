# Open Runner 🏃

A modern 3D endless runner game built with Three.js. Race through procedurally generated terrain, collect coins, avoid obstacles, and see how far you can go in this immersive browser-based adventure!

## 🎯 Game Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Procedurally Generated Terrain** - Every run is unique with dynamic landscape generation
- **Multiple Environments** - Forest and desert levels with distinct atmospheres and challenges
- **Progressive Difficulty** - Speed increases over time for escalating challenge
- **Score System** - Compete for high scores and see how far you can go

### Collectibles & Power-ups
- **Coins** - Collect golden coins to increase your score
- **Magnet Power-ups** - Attract nearby coins automatically
- **Score Doubler** - Temporarily double your coin collection value
- **Invisibility** - Pass through obstacles temporarily

### Enemies & Obstacles
- **Forest Enemies**: Bears, Squirrels, and Deer with unique behaviors
- **Desert Enemies**: Coyotes, Rattlesnakes, and Scorpions with different attack patterns
- **Environmental Hazards**: Trees, rocks, cacti, logs, and tumbleweeds

## 🎯 How to Play

### Controls

**Desktop Controls:**
- **A/D** or **Arrow Keys** - Turn left/right
- **ESC** - Pause game
- **R** - Restart after game over
- **F** - Toggle FPS counter

**Mobile Controls:**
- **Left/Right Buttons** - Turn the player
- **Pause Button** - Pause the game

### Gameplay Tips
- **Collect Coins**: Each coin = 10 points (doubled with power-ups)
- **Avoid Obstacles**: Collision with enemies or obstacles ends the game
- **Use Power-ups**: Pick up special items for temporary advantages
- **Progress Through Levels**: Reach score milestones to unlock desert level

## 🌍 Game Environments

### Forest Level 
- Rolling green hills with dense vegetation
- Enemies: Bears, Squirrels, and Deer
- Obstacles: Pine trees, fallen logs, rocks

### Desert Level
- Sandy, arid landscape with scattered cacti
- Enemies: Coyotes, Rattlesnakes, and Scorpions  
- Obstacles: Saguaro cacti, barrel cacti, desert rocks
